About Me

I am a 3rd year Computer Science Undergraduate Student. Two qualities that embody me are hard work and being detail-oriented. My drive and fire is ignited by my passion to create and develop and one thing I can vouch for is my work ethic. Also my mind kind of works on the principle that "Interpretation is Power and Vision is Success".

I am currently working at an IIT Bombay tech startup (GRROOM) to build an Artificial Intelligent Software for fashion styling and recommendation. The application is soon to be out on AWS Cloud and Playstore. In 2019, I got my summer training at University of Warwick, UK where I learnt about the Statistics behind Data Science and Machine Learning in R under Dr. Florin Ciucu (Associate Professor, Univeristy of Warwick).
